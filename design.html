From time to time I manage to design apps and other artefacts, amid the chaos of my Ph.D. studies. I'm a designer and a design researcher, doing my Ph.D. in HCDE at the University of Washington/Seattle, advised by Gary Hsieh and Sean Munson. I conduct my research as a member of the Prosocial Computing Lab and I teach HCI and Design to Bachelor and Master's students. Prior to the Ph.D., I worked in industry for 5 years, read my full CV here. You can catch me on Twitter, LinkedIn, Github, and Designer News, or send an email.
